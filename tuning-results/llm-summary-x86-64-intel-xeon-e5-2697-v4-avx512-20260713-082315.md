  - `vectorization`: SIMD efficiency and packed vector instructions vs scalar fallbacks. Events: `cycles,instructions,fp_arith_inst_retired.scalar_double,fp_arith_inst_retired.vector_256_double,fp_arith_inst_retired.vector_512_double`
